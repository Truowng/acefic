Mô tả công việc

A solution-oriented team player with a customer-centric mind and technical leadership.

What we expect you to perform:

  • Understand a project’s scope and create documents for project requirements
  • Work with clients to report analyzed data and provide project updates on a consistent basis about strategy, planning, adjustments, progress, blockers, etc.
  • Manage the project estimating activities
  • Coordinate schedule of activities related to the project
  • Monitor projects on a regular basis, evaluate progress and quality and manage issue resolution process to ensure corrective actions are taken
  • Maintain the quality of all project documents/data to assure the integrity of a project

  • Have good communication skills to work effectively with customers and the development team
  • Have analytical, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills
